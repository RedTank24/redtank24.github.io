About Me

My name is Benjamin Schmaal.

Ever since my childhood I've taken a lot of interest into being creative. Drawing and storywriting have always kept me occupied, but so have games: as such I hope to make my own story games one day.

Currently studying Creative Technology at the University of Twente, I'm a curious person that always wants to produce high quality works. Having had experience in storywriting, art and programming as a hobby, I hope to make my own worlds, stories and games one day.

Other skills such as game design, video editing and composition are within my reach, though I'm eager to add more to my skillset. Whilst groupwork isn't my strongside — often opting to work alone instead — I try my best to be as good in every field as possible to bring forth a product of good quality.
